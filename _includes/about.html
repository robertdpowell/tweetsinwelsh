About Me

Welcome to Sgwrs Cymraeg! I am a passionate language enthusiast committed to helping learners around the world improve their Welsh language skills. My mission is to provide you with engaging, authentic content every day that not only challenges you but also sparks your curiosity and love for the Welsh language and culture.

My Story

Sgwrs Cymraeg was created by a Welsh language learner who saw a need for a platform that could offer an opportunity for learners to practice and improve their Welsh in a fun and engaging way. I believe that language learning is most effective when it is contextual, meaningful, and relevant to the learner's life.

What I Offer

I provide daily dialogues in Welsh accompanied by translations in English, allowing you to immerse yourself in authentic Welsh conversations. The dialogues cover a wide range of topics, from everyday life and culture to more specialized subjects, ensuring that you always have something interesting and relevant to learn.

Key Features:

  • Authentic Dialogues: Dialogues are carefully crafted to reflect real-life conversations in Welsh, helping you to learn the language as it is spoken by native speakers.
  • Daily Updates: I upload new dialogues every day, ensuring that you always have fresh content to engage with.
  • Toggle Translations: With the easy-to-use toggle translations feature, you can reveal or hide translations at any time, allowing you to customise your learning experience based on your current language proficiency.
