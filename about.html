My wife and I have chosen to put our young children through Welsh medium education despite being non-Welsh speakers ourselves. Of course this means that learning Welsh is now a whole family adventure, and I'm always on the lookout for resources that can help us to keep up with our kids learning pace!

We both work full-time so don't have a great deal of time to invest in learning a new language, which I suspect is a common challenge for parents like us.

So, I built this site as an experiment to see to what extent I can improve my Welsh reading skills and vocabulary through daily translation of a bitesize piece of everyday Welsh.

I hope it adds some value for you.
